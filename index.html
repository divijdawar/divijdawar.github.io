About

I use this as a documentation of my learnings, a hub of cool resources, and to write down my thinking and ideas which I can look back upon. I think of myself as a generally curious person and have multiple interests at the same time. Right now I spend my time programming GPUs, reading philosophy, understanding Linux, and tinkering with cryptocurrencies like Monero.

I enjoy working on hard problems. I am not gifted nor possess any advanced technical knowledge, but I like working on problems that force me to think for long hours and explore resources and trajectories.

I spend a lot of time pondering about the future and what it would look like. I want to work on ideas that would likely not materialize if I weren't working on them. In that regard, I like to think of myself as a contrarian.
