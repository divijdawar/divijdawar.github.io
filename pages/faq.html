FAQ

Q: What if I see something incorrect in the post?

Q: Can I translate your posts to another language?

A: Yes, I update my old posts periodically. Sometimes I post half-written blogs in hopes of getting back at them later.
Q: What are some of my hobbies?

Q: What do you post about?
A: Most of my posts will be technical deep dives into topics in AI. Occasionally I might post about philosophy.
